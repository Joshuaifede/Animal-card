Lion

Scientific Name: Panthera leo

Facts: The lion is the only cat that lives in groups, which are called prides.

Elephant

Scientific Name: Loxodonta

Facts: Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth.

Tiger

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris

Facts: Tigers are excellent swimmers and can swim up to 6 kilometers.

Giraffe

Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis

Facts: Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth, with legs alone being taller than many humans.

Zebra

Scientific Name: Equus zebra

Facts: Zebras have a unique stripe pattern, and no two zebras have the same stripes.
